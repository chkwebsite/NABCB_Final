About NABCB

India's Premier
Accreditation Authority

The National Accreditation Board for Certification Bodies provides accreditation to Inspection, Certification and Validation & Verification Bodies based on an assessment of their competence, in accordance with International Standards and Guidelines.

Accreditation Schemes

Internationally recognized programmes aligned with ISO/IEC standards.

ISO/IEC 17021-1:2015
Management Systems

Conformity assessment requirements for bodies providing audit and certification of management systems including QMS, EMS, ISMS and more.

📦
ISO/IEC 17065:2012
Product / Process Certification

Conformity assessment requirements for bodies certifying products, processes and services, ensuring consistent quality for consumers and industry.

IAF MLA · APAC MRA More info →
👥
ISO/IEC 17024:2012
Certification of Persons

General requirements for bodies operating certification schemes for persons, ensuring competent professionals across all sectors in India.

IAF MLA · APAC MRA More info →

ISO/IEC 17020:2012
Inspection Bodies

Requirements for the operation of various types of bodies performing inspection, ensuring competence, impartiality and consistent methodology.
